Welcome to Sundown SMP


An 18+ vanilla SMP server valuing talent, creativity, and community engagement in its members.


Server Overview

Sundown SMP is a small, chill Vanilla Survival Multiplayer (SMP) Server. We prioritize preservation of the Minecraft experience, meaning there's no annoying in game command-based economy replacing what diamonds are perfectly capable of providing while encouraging community engagement and socialization. Our community's top priority is to provide a safe space for all players.

Server Features

Creativity

We are a community that possesses great appreciation in creativity and artistic talent. Talent is not a requirement to join, but it is an asset that will improve your sense of belonging in the community!

Technical Reliability

While technical issues always reserve the ability to arise, the server runs in a dockerized environment where automatic world backups are performed every two hours. Performance is great because this environment is optimized for hosting.

Vanilla

We like to preserve the authentic Minecraft experience. That means no weird command-based economies, teleporting systems, or heavy mods unfaithful to the vanilla experience. The diamond economy is the best economy!

All Styles Welcome

Whether your a builder, redstoner, technical farm builder, or PVPer, your skills combined with your respect and engagement secure you a seat here.

Community and Support

The server has Simple Voice Chat installed, and players are encouraged (but not forced!) to use it. It also has Geyser and Floodgate installed; allowing it to support both Java and Bedrock players.

Bluemap

Bluemap allows players and non-players to view a 3D map of the server from inside any browser. If you'd like to see this map, click here.

Safety

Safety is our priority. We have zero tolerance for behavior or speech that undermines our community. Rules apply to both the SMP and Discord; discrimination and hate-speech results in an immediate, permanent ban. We rely on mutual accountability to keep this space comfortable. If you’re here to contribute to a safe environment, welcome!

Maturity

Our server is 18+. Please note that until we happen to specify that there are no minors in the server, some of our members are minors. However, we are no longer accepting minors and are strict that all new applicants must be 18 or older without exception. The server will have a few 16+ minors until they either turn 18 or leave at their free will.

Frequently Asked Questions

How old do I have to be to join?

To join Sundown SMP, you must be 18+. However, explicit content is forbidden, and will result in a ban. Note that we recently transitioned to 18+ on a soft policy, so some members are 16+ until they either turn 18, or leave willingly.

What plugins are on the server?

The server has AxGraves (a graves plugin for preserving items after death), Bluemap (a web map), DiscordSRV (Minecraft chat is bridged into a Discord channel), Geyser and Floodgate (Bedrock client support), interactive chat, SimpleAFK, ViaBackwards and ViaVersion (for universal version support), and Simple Voice Chat (proximity chat).

Is voicechat required?

Voicechat is not required, but it's encouraged. Bedrock players will be unable to use voicechat, as it only supports Java clients.

How do I apply?

Start by clicking the "Join" button at the top of your page, then follow the onboarding instructions.

When will I be accepted

Applications are usually reviewed within one hour, but rarely can take up to 24. If your application isn't responded to within 24 hours, contact support at the bottom of the page.

What do I do if I face technical difficulties

Reach out to support via the Discord tickets system, or by contacting anybody listed in the staff section at the bottom of the page.

Is Discord required to join the server?

Yes. Discord acts as both a communication platform for members to socialize, and a platform for staff to maintain a clear record of server members. You must be a member of the Discord server to join the SMP.

How do I join on console?

See this tutorial. If you're still unable to join after following the video's instructions, notify a staff member.
